Choosing the Right Font for Articles

A guide to selecting the perfect typography for your content

Professional Recommendation

For long-form articles, serif fonts are generally preferred as they guide the eye along lines of text and improve readability.

For online articles, sans-serif fonts can work well too, especially with proper spacing and sizing.

Typography Best Practices for Articles

• Use 16-20px font size for body text on websites

• Maintain line height of 1.5-1.8 for readability

• Limit line length to 50-75 characters

• Ensure good contrast between text and background

• Use larger sizes for headings with a clear hierarchy
